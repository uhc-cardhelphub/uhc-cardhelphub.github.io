How to activate your UHC card (Unofficial guide)

Before you start, have your UHC card, your date of birth, and the ZIP code on file ready. For security-sensitive actions like activation, always use the official site: activate.uhc.com.

  1. Go to activate.uhc.com in your browser.
  2. Enter the requested details exactly as they appear in your plan records.
  3. Create or sign in to your account if prompted. Use a unique, strong password.
  4. Complete the verification steps and confirm activation.
  5. Sign out when finished, especially on shared devices.

Replace a lost, stolen, or damaged card

Log in to your official UHC member account or call the member services number on the back of your card. Avoid sharing your full member ID through email or text.

  • Update your mailing address before requesting a new card.
  • Ask about a digital ID card in the UHC app as a temporary option.
  • Dispose of damaged cards by cutting through magnetic stripes and QR codes.

Understand your UHC card details

Typical items include your Member ID, Group Number, plan type (e.g., PPO/HMO), and copay lines for office, urgent care, and ER. Providers use these numbers to verify coverage.

  • Carry your card or keep the digital version handy in the official UHC app.
  • Share your Member ID only with your provider or UHC support.
